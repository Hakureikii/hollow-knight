WHAT IS HOLLOW
KNIGHT?
Hollow Knight is a challenging 2D
action-adventure. You’ll explore
twisting caverns, battle tainted
creatures and escape intricate traps,
all to solve an ancient long-hidden
mystery.
Beneath the fading town of Dirtmouth
sleeps a vast, ancient kingdom.
Many are drawn beneath the
surface, searching for riches, or
glory, or answers to old secrets.
As the enigmatic Knight, you’ll
traverse the depths, unravel its
mysteries and conquer its evils.
